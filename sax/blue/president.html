President

Mr. Mulbah Gbozee (President) is a candidate for Masterâ€™s of Science degree in Civil Engineering (Material Science) at the Central South University in Changsha, Hunan Province. He obtained a Bachelor of Science degree in Civil Engineering from the Stella Maris Polytechnic in Liberia.
Before becoming President, Mr. Gbozee served as Vice President of the Union from July 2015-July 2016. He also served in various capacities including member of Nanjing 2014 Conference secretariat and member of the Planning and Program committee of 2015. While pursuing his undergraduate degree, he served as Secretary General for the Civil and Architecture Students Association (CASA) of Stella Maris Polytechnic and later as Vice President for the Student Representative Council (SRC) of Stella Maris Polytechnic.
In Liberia, President Gbozee worked as a Project Engineer on major engineering projects amongst which were: The rehabilitation of the 245km rail track that connects the port of Buchanan to the Iron ore mine at Mt. Tokadeh and the Rehabilitation the Red-light to Gbarnga Road. In both of these projects, he planned and directed construction activities.
