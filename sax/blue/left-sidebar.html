About The Liberian Student Union in China


  The Liberian Student Union in China (LSUIC) was established July 2006 as a student-based group. The focal point of the organization is to provide an effective and efficient platform to enhance participation among Liberian Students in China. Against this backdrop, the group was organized to ensure the integration of common goals and objectives so as to provide a unique learning and cultural experience for Liberian students’ while in pursuit of academic “excellence through hard work” in the People’s Republic of China. LSUIC mission is to cultivate and maintain the morale of students, as well as, providing an amicable learning atmosphere for achievement and success. The union promotes affiliation among its members in spite of social, cultural, sexual, and political orientation. “LIBERIAN STUDENT UNION IN CHINA”
